Degree and Certficate Programs

Save money by completing your first two years at RVC. Whether you're pursuing an A.A.S. degree or a degree requiring more than two years, Rock Valley College has plans for everyone. Get an Associate in Applied Science or Certificate in one of many career propgrams including Accounting, Business, Nursing, Criminal Justice, Web Programming and Design, and many more.

Rock Valley College also makes it possible to earn college credits while being in high school. Get a head start on college by taking dual credit courses at RVC or dual credit courses at your high school. Even earn your associates degree at the same time as your high school diploma through the Running Start Program. RVC also accepts many AP credits.

Athletics

Join one of RVC's athleic programs, including basketball, softball, bowling, soccer, volleyball, and baseball. Just looking to have fun in a casual environment? Play ultimate frisbee, futsal, dodge ball, or ping-pong in our intramural sports program.

Get Involved

Interested in getting involved in a student organization? Join one of our many clubs of students with your intersts. Japanese multimedia and culture club, student government, peer mentoring, RVC students of service organization, and even e-sports are just some of the clubs/organizations you could be a part of.

Starlight Theatre

Study theatre at RVC in one of our 15 transferable theatre courses, with classes covering acting, diretcing, costuming, stage makeup, and many more. Audition or volunteer to be a part of a Starlight play. Starlight Theatre provides a creative outlet for both students and the community.

Academics

Rock Valley provides an affordable experience for everyone. We have a bookstore, computer and math labs, a library, tutoring and writing centers, and many more on campus facilities. Check out Degrees and Certificates for more information.
